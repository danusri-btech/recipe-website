Dosa

Preparation Time: 20 minutes (excluding fermentation)

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for 6–8 hours.
  2. Grind into a smooth batter by adding water.
  3. Allow the batter to ferment overnight.
  4. Heat a pan and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour a ladle of batter and spread it in a circular motion.
  6. Drizzle oil and cook until golden brown.
  7. Fold and serve hot with chutney or sambar.
