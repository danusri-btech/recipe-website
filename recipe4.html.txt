<section class="recipe">
    <h2>Fresh Vegetable Salad</h2>

    <img src="images/salad.jpg" alt="Salad">

    <p><strong>Preparation Time:</strong> 15 minutes</p>
    <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy</p>

    <h3>Ingredients</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>1 cucumber (chopped)</li>
        <li>2 tomatoes (chopped)</li>
        <li>1 onion (sliced)</li>
        <li>1 carrot (grated)</li>
        <li>Fresh lettuce leaves</li>
        <li>Salt (to taste)</li>
        <li>Black pepper</li>
        <li>1 tbsp lemon juice</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Instructions</h3>
    <ol>
        <li>Wash all vegetables thoroughly.</li>
        <li>Chop cucumber, tomatoes, and onion.</li>
        <li>Grate the carrot.</li>
        <li>Add all vegetables to a large bowl.</li>
        <li>Sprinkle salt and black pepper.</li>
        <li>Add lemon juice and mix well.</li>
        <li>Serve fresh.</li>
    </ol>
