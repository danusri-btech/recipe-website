<section class="recipe">
    <h2>Vegetable Soup</h2>

    <img src="images/soup.jpg" alt="Soup">

    <p><strong>Preparation Time:</strong> 30 minutes</p>
    <p><strong>Difficulty:</strong> Easy</p>

    <h3>Ingredients</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>1 cup chopped carrots</li>
        <li>1 cup chopped beans</li>
        <li>1/2 cup corn</li>
        <li>1 chopped onion</li>
        <li>2 cloves garlic</li>
        <li>4 cups vegetable broth or water</li>
        <li>Salt (to taste)</li>
        <li>Black pepper</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Instructions</h3>
    <ol>
        <li>Heat a pan and sauté onion and garlic.</li>
        <li>Add chopped vegetables and cook for a few minutes.</li>
        <li>Pour in vegetable broth or water.</li>
        <li>Bring to a boil, then simmer for 15–20 minutes.</li>
        <li>Add salt and pepper to taste.</li>
        <li>Serve hot.</li>
    </ol>
